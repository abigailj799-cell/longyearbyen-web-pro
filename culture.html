Longyearbyen was originally founded by American John Munro Longyear, and was founded on the existance of coal, influencing settling patterns. The cableway towers placed around are how the coal was transported throughout the town to the harbor. The locals, while not using the towers to transport coal anymore, use them as resting and stopping spots.

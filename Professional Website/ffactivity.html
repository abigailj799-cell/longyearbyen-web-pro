Family hike and tour that everyone is able to do, with fun activities for both kids and adults lasting around 4-5 hours. Depending on the season, kids can do a variety of activities, including:

  • Scavenger Hunt
  • Nature Observation (making a journal)
  • Body and Mind Arctic Challenges (small)
  • Playing in the Snow (when possible)
  • Indoor activities
Safety, snowshoes, spikes, head lamps, and side activity equipment are all provided.
